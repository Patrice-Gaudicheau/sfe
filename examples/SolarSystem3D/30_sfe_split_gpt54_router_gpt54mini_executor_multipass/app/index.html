About

Use mouse, trackpad, or touch to orbit the scene. Click a body to focus it and update the information panel. Keyboard shortcuts: Space toggles play/pause, L toggles labels, O toggles orbits, R toggles realistic scale mode, 1–5 change camera presets, and S cycles season presets.

Seasons are shown as an educational approximation: Earth's seasons come from its axial tilt relative to sunlight, not from being closer to or farther from the Sun.
